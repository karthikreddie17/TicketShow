Welcome to your User dashboard!

Here you can easily book your tickets and manage your bookings. To book a ticket, simply click on the "Ticket Booking" link and you'll be taken to our ticket booking page. From there, you can choose the date, time, and number of tickets you want to book, and complete the payment process. If you want to check your previous bookings, simply click on the "Bookings" link and you'll be able to see all your past and upcoming bookings. Enjoy your experience on our platform and feel free to contact us if you have any questions or concerns!
